Why visit

A slower coast with summer sunshine

Sri Lanka’s East Coast is especially useful when the south-west monsoon affects the southern beaches. It offers a different coastal mood, with long beaches, warmer seas, local culture and more space.

Trincomalee, Pasikudah and Arugam Bay each suit different travellers, from families and couples to surfers and slow coastal explorers.

Good to know

FAQs about East Coast

When is the best time for the East Coast?

May to September is usually the strongest period for the East Coast, especially compared with the south-west coast.

Which is better: Trincomalee, Pasikudah or Arugam Bay?

Trincomalee is good for culture and marine life, Pasikudah for calmer resort stays, and Arugam Bay for surf and a casual beach-town feel.

Is the East Coast good for families?

Yes, especially areas such as Pasikudah where calmer waters and resort-style stays can suit families.
