Why visit

Tea country, temples and mountain scenery

Kandy and the Hill Country offer a cooler, greener side of Sri Lanka. This region is known for the Temple of the Tooth, scenic train routes, tea plantations, colonial-era bungalows and dramatic mountain landscapes.

It pairs naturally with the Cultural Triangle before travelling onwards to Ella, Yala or the southern coast.

Good to know

FAQs about Kandy & Hill Country

Is the scenic train worth it?

Yes, it is one of Sri Lanka’s most memorable experiences, especially between Kandy, Nuwara Eliya and Ella.

How cold is the Hill Country?

It is cooler than the coast, especially in the evenings. A light jacket is useful.

How many nights should I spend in the Hill Country?

Three to five nights gives you time for Kandy, tea country and scenic travel without rushing.
