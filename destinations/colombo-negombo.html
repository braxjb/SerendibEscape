Why visit

The easy gateway into Sri Lanka

Colombo and Negombo are ideal for travellers who want a soft landing after arrival or a comfortable final night before departure. The area combines city life, coastal views, food, shopping, temples and colonial heritage.

This region works well at the start or end of a tailor-made itinerary, especially when flight times make it useful to stay close to the airport.

Good to know

FAQs about Colombo & Negombo

Should I stay in Colombo or Negombo after arrival?

Negombo is easier for airport access, while Colombo is better for city culture, restaurants and shopping.

How long should I spend here?

One or two nights is usually enough unless you want a deeper city experience.

Is Colombo worth visiting?

Yes, especially with a good guide. It offers food, markets, temples, galleries and colonial history.
