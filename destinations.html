Island of contrasts

A small island with beaches, wildlife, culture, tea country and ancient history.

Sri Lanka is ideal for travellers who want variety without long internal flights. Within one journey, guests can explore UNESCO heritage sites, travel through green hill country, watch elephants and leopards in national parks, relax on the coast, and experience warm local hospitality.

Good to know

Sri Lanka travel FAQs

How many days are enough for Sri Lanka?

Around 10 to 14 days is a good duration for a balanced trip. Shorter trips can focus on one or two regions.

Is Sri Lanka good for families?

Yes. Wildlife, beaches, cultural activities and short travel distances make it suitable for family holidays.

What is the best way to travel around Sri Lanka?

A private car with a chauffeur-guide is usually the easiest and most comfortable option for tailor-made journeys.

When is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka is a year-round destination. The west and south coasts are best from December to April, while the east coast is ideal from May to September.
